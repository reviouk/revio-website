A New Responsive eCommerce Site for Pushkin Antiques

30 July 2014

Step 1: Research

We engaged in detailed client consultation and analysed competitor websites alongside feedback from existing customers regarding the previous site. The objective centred on designing a streamlined, contemporary platform emphasising product photography and engagement mechanisms to attract prospective buyers and sellers, while maintaining alignment with the client's established brand identity and eBay marketplace presence.

Step 2: First a Sketch of the Proposed Layout

Initial conceptual sketches emerged from the client meeting, addressing layout considerations, visual elements, and structural organisation. Preliminary sketches covered the homepage, with additional drawings developed for supplementary pages including the shop, services, about, and contact sections.

Step 3: Hi-Res Mockup of the Site

Before development commenced, comprehensive mockups and wireframe documentation were created across all pages. Following client approval, the project advanced to the web development phase, establishing a test environment and initiating code development.

Step 4: A Responsive Website for Mobile, Desktop and Tablet

The completed deliverable: a responsive eCommerce site built on the WordPress and WooCommerce platform.
