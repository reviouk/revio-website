How to Use Instagram Part 1 – How to Take Pictures Using Instagram

21 September 2012

Instagram: Installation

Begin by accessing either the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and downloading the Instagram application.

Taking Photos

Instagram offers two approaches to capturing and editing images: photograph first then modify, or preview filters before shooting. We recommend framing your shot, capturing it, and adjusting afterward using filters.

To take a picture using Instagram, tap the camera icon.

1. Editing Options

Each box provides different image settings that transform the photograph's appearance.

2. The Insta-Change Button

This feature adjusts contrast, saturation, and other technical photo-editing elements, producing striking results. Once satisfied, click the checkmark to continue.

3. Sharing Your Photo

Maximise visibility by sharing across multiple platforms. After syncing your accounts, select the corresponding icons on the upload screen. Selected options appear highlighted in blue.

4. Your Picture is Now Live

Your image is successfully posted to Instagram.

Alternative Method

You can use your device's standard photo application, then process through Instagram. However, most phones capture rectangular images while Instagram requires square formats, necessitating cropping that may remove important content. Using the Instagram app directly is generally preferable.
