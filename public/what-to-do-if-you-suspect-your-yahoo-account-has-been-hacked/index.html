What to Do If You Suspect Your YAHOO account has been Hacked?

30 September 2016

Don't panic! Follow these steps if you have a YAHOO account.

  1. Change your password to a high strength secure password with letters numbers and characters.
  2. Check your alternative email address is correct.

Your YAHOO account may have been hacked if:

  • Your yahoo account information/details has changed without your knowledge.
  • There are logins from locations you don't recognise on your recent activity page.
  • You aren't receiving expected emails from senders
  • Your Yahoo Mail account is sending spam.

Full details here: https://help.yahoo.com/kb/SLN2090.html
