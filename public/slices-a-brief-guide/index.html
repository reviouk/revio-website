Slices for Twitter: a brief taster

19 September 2012

The best things often come in slices – like pizza, birthday cake, but when it comes to social media for Twitter, how sweet is Slice for Twitter.

So What Is Slice for Twitter?

We all know that if you're following a handful of fellow tweeters, your Twitter stream can easily be overloaded with noise. Slice is a cool app for Twitter (for mobile and web) that allows you to de-clutter your message stream. It works by tapping into the Twitter directory and allows you to view tweets categorised into different verticals, so you can organise your tweets for easier reading by topic, with no need to prune the many followed. It's a similar principle to Twitter Lists, but infinitely more "uber cool".

Easy to install. After authorising the app for your Twitter account, it automatically created slices for celebrity, travel and tv/movies. Not remembering which celebs I'd followed, I checked and saw Charlie Sheen & Charlie Brooker in my "celeb slice".

Features

  • Supports up to five different Twitter accounts
  • Allows posting to Facebook
  • Ability to filter out or mute certain users, hashtags, and keywords
  • Free version with ads; paid version available
  • Upcoming stats function showing top tweeters, mentions, trending topics, and retweets by slice
  • Compatible with Android and iOS devices
