The 10 Step Roadmap for Implementing HubSpot Marketing & Sales Platform

22 July 2018

1. Initial HubSpot Consulting Call

Schedule a complimentary 20-minute consultation with a HubSpot consultant to:

  • Establish mutual understanding through introductions
  • Evaluate mission goals and relevant HubSpot capabilities
  • Develop target personas and ideal lead profiles
  • Review existing marketing strategies and lead generation initiatives
  • Assess team expertise and composition
  • Evaluate current HubSpot experience, if any
  • Identify required HubSpot tool requirements
  • Walk through a typical implementation roadmap
  • Plan next steps toward inbound marketing achievement

2. Define Goals & Objectives

Establish measurable success criteria for your inbound strategy:

  • Review the HubSpot inbound methodology
  • Establish quantifiable objectives (e.g., 25% lead increase)
  • Identify where potential buyers research companies
  • Document marketing challenges
  • Define ideal customer profile
  • Project program ROI outcomes

3. Review & Map Journey's Processes

Optimise where HubSpot improves inbound and sales operations:

  • Map customer journey touchpoints across ideal website experiences
  • Document sales process workflows
  • Create lead status classifications
  • Establish deal stages and sales pipelines

4. HubSpot Initial Setup

Execute technical configuration and system connections:

  • Activate lead tracking functionality
  • Add team users to the platform
  • Implement tracking code across digital properties
  • Integrate CRM systems
  • Import existing contact and lead databases
  • Connect social media channels
  • Configure HubSpot blog or external blog integration
  • Filter out internal company traffic

5. Training Part I

Introduce team members to HubSpot dashboard navigation and available tool capabilities.
