Junglepay from txtNation – No Monkey Business with a Winning Mobile Billing Solution

6 October 2011

In today's evolving marketing landscape, mobile SMS messaging offers a streamlined approach to online payments and billing via mobile devices, benefiting both users and businesses.

With iPad and Android devices reshaping consumer interaction and engagement—from app purchases to gaming, music, and movies—mobile payment gateways and automated billing solutions enable businesses to monetize digital assets and process transactions securely.

Junglepay, developed by txtNation, provides a plug-and-play integration widget enabling seamless integration with existing websites and mobile applications. It facilitates payments through SMS, credit card, or telephone, leveraging txtNation's platform and carrier partnerships to reach global markets via premium SMS, landline, and credit card options. Setup is free and designed for simplicity using an intuitive configuration wizard.

"With JunglePay, it's easier than ever to set up SMS billing with minimal effort and low overheads. Premium SMS is ideal for targeting services at young social networkers, the unbanked, and those without credit cards." — Michael Whelan, txtNation

As social gaming and networking applications expand, JunglePay positions businesses to deliver the features customers desire: convenience, ease of use, and flexibility.
