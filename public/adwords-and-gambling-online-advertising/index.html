AdWords and Gambling Online Advertising

15 September 2009

From January 2009, Google lifted its ban on advertising online gambling sites through AdWords in the UK and other countries. This shift opened paid advertising opportunities on AdWords for online poker and bingo websites.

Google's policy change regarding PPC gambling advertising potentially generates substantial revenue for the company during an economically challenging period. However, this development may reduce opportunities for content creators and copywriters, as gambling sites had previously relied on distributing fresh content and articles for visibility.

To advertise on Google AdWords, online casinos and betting operations must establish an AdWords account and complete legal declarations. Advertising is permitted when targeting only UK audiences, which Google's geo-targeting feature facilitates.

Requirements for gambling site advertising

  • Register your business with the gambling commission organisation.
  • Target only users aged 18 and over.
  • Display a prominent link to gamblingaware.co.uk or gamcare.org.uk on your website.
