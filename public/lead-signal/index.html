01 — How it works

Capture every DM. Qualify with AI. Book on autopilot.

Your channels and CRM stay exactly as they are. LeadSignal is the intelligence layer that turns a flood of conversations into booked calls — in four moves.

→ Capture

Every channel, one pipeline

Instagram & Facebook DMs stream in through ManyChat; YouTube comments via the Enterprise add-on. No copy-paste, no new inbox.

→ Qualify

Scored like your best closer

Every chat scored 0–100 with a tier, plain-English summary, intent signals, objections and a recommended next action.

→ Autopilot

The AI works the conversation

It qualifies and drives the booking automatically, drafting on-brand replies — and hands off to a human the instant it matters.

→ Attribute

All the way to revenue

Booked calls and won deals sync back from your CRM & Calendly, so you see booking rate and revenue by source.

02 — The platform

Everything your DM sales team needs, in one pipeline.

Lead scoring & tiers

Every lead 0–100 with a HOT / WARM / NURTURE / LOW / DISQUALIFIED tier and a one-line read — so the right leads rise to the top.

Signal Priority list

Unbooked leads sorted hottest-first, each with the AI summary and recommended next action. The screen your team lives in.

AI Copilot

One click drafts the perfect next message in your voice — moving the lead toward the call without staring at a blank box.

AI Sales Coach

Reviews the whole deal — the DM plus score, objections, funnel status and live CRM signals — flags deal leaks and the next best move, and writes the coaching back to your CRM.

Attribution & revenue

Every lead carries its source from first DM to closed £. See booking rate and revenue by channel — and finally know what actually pays.

Your CRM, connected

Two-way with GoHighLevel or HubSpot, plus native Calendly. Booked calls and won deals sync back, and each lead's tier is written into the CRM so setters get prioritised lists there too.

Which channels does it cover?

Instagram and Facebook on every plan, in one pipeline. On Enterprise you can add YouTube comment monitoring (+$99/mo); a LinkedIn add-on for B2B teams is coming soon.

How does booking get tracked?

Booked calls sync from your CRM and from Calendly natively — so booking-rate and attribution stay accurate.

Revio deploys and configures it for your funnel — scoring rubric tuned to your offer and reporting your team will actually use. Typically live within two weeks.
