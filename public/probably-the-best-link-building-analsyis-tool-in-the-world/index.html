Probably The Best Link Building Analsyis Tool in The World!

9 February 2013

Digital marketing analysts and link building enthusiasts constantly search for superior link analysis solutions. Several tools have emerged over time, though Ahrefs appears to be the most powerful option available. Their Site Explorer stands out as a premier resource.

Why Ahrefs Leads the Market

  1. Extensive Database – Ahrefs has the largest database of live links and is updated frequently. This currency proves essential for tracking competitor SEO activities.
  2. Thorough Backlink Analysis – The platform delivers comprehensive backlink and referring domain examination.
  3. Smart Anchor Text Reporting – Rather than calculating anchor text based solely on volume, the system presents percentages of referring domains using specific anchor phrases, preventing site-wide links from skewing results.
  4. Visual Reports – Comprehensive graphical presentations allow quick assessments at a glance.
  5. Technical Auditing – The tool identifies issues including timeouts, missing title tags, and duplicate titles (though less detailed than Google Webmaster Tools).
  6. Link Movement Tracking – "New and Lost" link reports with timestamps help identify link loss patterns and underlying causes.
  7. Historical Analysis – Reports spanning thirty days reveal H1/H2 headers, canonical problems, and additional metrics.

Conclusion

While numerous SEO tools exist, Ahrefs excels at detailed backlink analysis – particularly valuable following Penguin and Panda algorithm updates.
