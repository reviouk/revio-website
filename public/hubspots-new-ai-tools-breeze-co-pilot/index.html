The Breeze Prospecting Agent takes the manual labour out of prospecting by automating key tasks. It starts by analysing companies and contacts in your CRM that match your target persona, ensuring that your sales team focuses on high-quality leads with a higher likelihood of conversion.

But the Prospecting Agent doesn't stop there. It goes the extra mile by researching each target company and contact, developing a deep understanding of their business needs. This level of personalisation is crucial in today's sales landscape, where generic outreach often falls flat.

Personalized Outreach Strategies

Armed with this in-depth knowledge, the Breeze Prospecting Agent crafts highly personalised outreach messages. It leverages the context from your CRM and its own research to create emails that resonate with each prospect, increasing the chances of engagement and response.

This AI-powered expert doesn't just write the emails; it also knows when to send them. By looking for signals of intent and activity, the Prospecting Agent reaches out at the optimal time, maximising the impact of each interaction.

AI-Driven Pipeline Generation

Perhaps the most impressive aspect of the Breeze Prospecting Agent is its ability to work around the clock. This AI sales agent never sleeps, constantly searching for opportunities and engaging with prospects to keep your pipeline full.

You maintain control over the outreach process, with options for manual review or fully automated sending. This flexibility allows you to tailor the Prospecting Agent's actions to your team's workflow and preferences.

The result is a steady stream of qualified leads entering your pipeline, without the need for constant manual effort from your sales team. They can focus on what they do best — building relationships and closing deals — while the Breeze Prospecting Agent handles the initial legwork.

HubSpot's Breeze Co-Pilot, with its Prospecting Agent, is a game-changer for sales teams looking to boost their efficiency and effectiveness.
