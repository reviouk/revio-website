How to Create a Pro Marketing Video Without Breaking the Bank

2 June 2013

As a marketing vehicle, videos have been one of the best ways to market your company online for a while now, but it doesn't have to be an expensive proposition or animated.

A decent video can be created in a matter of a few hours, once you get the hang of it.

  1. Think of an idea/concept. Brainstorm with your friends, family and colleagues.
  2. Write the script. Create a simple storyboard and message. Keep it short and sweet, pointed with calls to action, and get feedback from your friends or an online community.
  3. Create the visuals. You can get stock images or videos online. If you're a reasonably good artist, produce some sketches on an electronic pen board like Bamboo — sped up, these can look quite cool. Live video can be a very good method to present yourself and your message. If you have a video camera or a decent video camera phone, use this, but you may need a tripod or stand to keep it steady. To screen record and add effects, get a copy of Camtasia Studio or ScreenFlow and get to work on your transitions.
  4. Record the voice over and/or download some music. Choose appropriate music or a jingle from stock music websites like AudioMicro or Freesound, or compose your own.
  5. Then publish it and upload it to YouTube and other sharing sites. Use Wistia to embed it easily within your website. It has a professional and smart media player with SEO-optimised features.
  6. Last but not least, carry out some social media outreach and get some 'play time'.
