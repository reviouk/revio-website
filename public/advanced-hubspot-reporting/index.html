Advanced HubSpot Reporting: Building Reports for Insights

29 September 2021

Overview

HubSpot's built-in reporting features have notable constraints. The platform cannot integrate external data sources, limiting comprehensive marketing analysis. Additionally, customisation depth and analytical capabilities lag behind tools like Google Sheets and Excel.

The reporting add-on improves functionality by creating centralised dashboards combining marketing and sales reports. However, limitations persist: multi-portal reporting remains unavailable, external data integration is impossible, and customisation remains cumbersome.

Solution: Third-Party Integration Tools

Enhancing HubSpot's analytics through complementary applications and integrations enables better data visualisation and extraction.

1. Wicked Reports

A first-party data attribution platform emphasising ROI-driven insights through transaction data. It automatically detects HubSpot leads, tracks customer lifetime value, and synchronises email events.

Key advantages over HubSpot attribution:

  • Detects subscription revenue attribution
  • Direct e-commerce integrations (Shopify, Stripe, PayPal, WooCommerce)
  • Provides data-driven playbooks
  • Reconciles with order systems rather than HubSpot deal revenue
  • Prevents revenue over-reporting
  • Offers performance benchmarking

2. Supermetrics

Centralises marketing and sales data from HubSpot, Google Analytics, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and LinkedIn into Google Sheets, Excel, or Google Data Studio dashboards. Supports multi-portal reporting and automated data refresh schedules.

Pricing: 14-day free trial; $99/month starting rate.

3. DashThis

Dashboard platform providing campaign KPI visualisation with template-based setup. Exports reports as PDFs for team sharing.

Offer: 10 dashboards available during 15-day free trial.

4. Databox

Integrates HubSpot with Google Analytics, social platforms, and marketing tools into unified dashboards. Features pre-configured templates, custom reporting, goal tracking, and metric notifications. Available on desktop, mobile, and Slack.

5. Power BI Integration

Enables HubSpot data extraction for aggregated reporting alongside ERP and accounting systems within Microsoft Power BI dashboards.

Capabilities:

  • Query and edit HubSpot data without database integrity impact
  • Consolidate key company metrics in single dashboard
  • Free templates available
  • Includes cloud-based server and data warehouse
