Services-Online.com O2 Vodaphone Three Email and SMS Scams

6 April 2021

Overview

Scammers are targeting individuals globally with SMS phishing attacks (smishing) impersonating major UK mobile carriers. Common messages claim: "We were unable to process your most recent bill. Your [carrier] services will be disabled unless your information is updated via [fraudulent URL]".

Red Flags to Recognise

  • It's asking you to enter bank details by providing a direct link
  • It's attempting to panic you into action by stating that O2 was unable to process your latest bill
  • The URL is suspicious, containing the domain "o2services-online" — this site is nothing to do with O2
  • Messages arrive from random numbers unassociated with legitimate carrier communications

Protection Steps

  1. Avoid clicking links in unsolicited messages
  2. Verify URLs against official domains (e.g., accounts.o2.co.uk, login.vodafone.co.uk)
  3. Block suspicious numbers and delete messages
  4. Forward scam texts to 7726 (free reporting service)
  5. Report to Action Fraud and carrier security pages
  6. Use device-native blocking features
