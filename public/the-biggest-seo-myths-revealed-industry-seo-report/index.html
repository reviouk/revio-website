The Biggest SEO Myths Revealed – Industry SEO Report

10 May 2010

SEO myths and reality – Rankability uncover some of the SEO industry advice and claims

There are a myriad of SEO companies and experts out there to choose from, and a mountain of information and articles about how to do SEO, but how do you differentiate the chaff from the wheat – the myths from reality!

Like any industry there is the good, the bad and the ugly! To help you decipher some of the SEO terminology and myths, we have highlighted some of the things to look out for when wading through your SEO proposals and choosing the right SEO company to manage your SEO campaigns.

Rankability have recently uncovered an invaluable SEO report that reveals the top 7 SEO myths, and how to avoid some of the less virtuous so-called SEO experts that do not want you to know the full picture.

Rob Wells, the managing director of Rankability states: "There is a kind of cloak or veil of secrecy around SEO and how it is done, and sometimes this is used to leverage an advantage over clients who do not possess the means or knowledge to determine what is being said has any credence or not."

He adds that the firm differs by ensuring transparency: "We are different in our approach to SEO in that we go to the trouble of explaining our processes and are fully transparent in our online marketing methods, the effort required to achieve your goals, where the budget is spent and the potential return on investment for your business."

Rankability hopes publishing this report will clarify SEO misconceptions and help online businesses understand that SEO represents a valuable marketing strategy for achieving business objectives.
