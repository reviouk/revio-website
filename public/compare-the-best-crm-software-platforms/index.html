Introduction

Selecting an appropriate CRM platform presents considerable challenges given the abundance of options available. A free CRM trial is often not long enough to work out the best fit for your business — it's only when you start using a CRM platform within your organisation that you realise some of the pros and cons.

This guide is designed to help you through a structured comparison process, and we offer a free template to make it easier. As a HubSpot agency partner having onboarded over 65 clients, Revio's consultants are experienced guides for CRM selection.

CRM Platform Reviews

HubSpot

HubSpot is a free CRM solution that you can start your business on. It offers relatively advanced features, though some are restricted to premium tiers. The platform integrates sales, marketing and service capabilities with user-friendly interfaces requiring configuration and training.

Salesforce

Salesforce is a mature CRM solution and maintains market leadership with extensive features and AI innovations. Its Sales Cloud component delivers sales automation tools, reporting, deal management and more, enabling organisations to handle leads, progress tracking and workflow automation.

Zoho CRM, Insightly and Pipedrive

These platforms round out the comparison and are worth evaluating alongside HubSpot and Salesforce depending on your budget and feature requirements.
